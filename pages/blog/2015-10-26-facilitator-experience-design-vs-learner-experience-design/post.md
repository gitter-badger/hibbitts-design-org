Key learner experience design goals:
* Engaging
* Convenient
* Organized
* Relevant
* Enjoyable

Key facilitator experience design goals (likely to supercede my earlier set of [Desired Qualities of a Flipped-LMS](../desired-qualities-of-a-flipped-lms-approach):
* Convenient
* Open
* Collaborative
* Enjoyable
* Pliable
